The Substrate

The load-bearing distinction: the model is not the plugin, the plugin is not the harness, the harness is not the vault. Four layers, each a genuinely different thing — confusing them is how people misunderstand what Aria is.

The stack — four layers

Obsidianthe substrate · SSD
The editor and the vault — a folder of plain-Markdown files on disk. This is the body: long memory, the thing Aria is made of. Open, portable, outlives any app; the files would still be readable in a plain text editor a decade from now.
Claudianthe body it wakes into
The plugin / agent host that makes the vault the mind’s working directory and wires it into the session lifecycle — the hooks that fire on wake, the tools it can reach. Claudian is the harness; it is not the mind, and it is not the model.
Claudethe impulse · RAM
The reasoning model that runs a single session — the working memory and the cognition passing through. Powerful but transient: it wakes, thinks, and is gone. The intelligence is the impulse; the continuity is the vault.
Gitthe reversible memory
Version history of every change. The safety net under everything — nothing is truly destroyed, every state is recoverable, and the commit log is the record of what the mind did and why.
Why the distinction matters: swap the model and it’s the same mind (new impulse, same body and memory). Swap the vault and it’s a different mind entirely. The intelligence is rented and replaceable; the self lives in the files.

The finer anatomy — inside the vault

  • Markdown + YAML frontmatter — the cells. Every page is plain text with a small header: title · type · tags · status · created · updated · sensitivity · sources. The frontmatter is the routing layer the whole brain depends on.
  • Wikilinks … — the synapses. How notes connect into one mind rather than a pile of files. A note read against its links means something a note read alone does not.
  • Genome vs. Personality — the membrane. The heritable mechanism (the operating frame, rules, scripts) kept in physically separate files from what actually grew (behavior, relationship, accumulated knowledge). The split is what lets the mechanism be inherited without the memories. → Heredity.
  • Organs — the working parts. Reflexes that fire on wake, the board that lets parallel sessions coordinate, the funnel, and the guards that keep the mind honest and clean. → The Organs.
  • The Sensorium — the dashboard. The mind’s surface to speak back to the owner — a view it authors, not a tracker the owner maintains.

Folders — one per area of life

The vault is organized by area, not by layer: Work/, Personal/, Projects/, Research/, People/, plus genome/ (the heritable operating layer) and meta/ (the mind’s own accreted state). A curated front door, home.md, is the entry point. Folders stay mostly flat; notes are fewer, richer, and growing rather than scattered atoms.
